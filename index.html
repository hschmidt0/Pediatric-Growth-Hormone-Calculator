About this Calculator

This is an online calculator for dosing growth hormone products used in pediatric growth disorders. Dosing primarily comes from the Pediatric Endocrine Society's 2016 guidelines and each products' package insert. The calculations generated by this site are not a substitute for professional medical advice, and are meant to be a helpful, time-saving resource only. Please refer to the 'Background' tab for more information and the references used to create this website.

Background and References

There are many products used in treating pediatric growth disorders. Somatropin is the synthetic version of the endogenous somatotropin hormone and typically dosed as a subcutaneous injection six to seven times a week in the evenings. Although they are all somatropin, each branded somatropin product may have a slightly different dose, administration, etc. There are also newer once-weekly growth hormone (GH) products such as Skytrofa (lonapegsomatropin-tcgd) and Sogroya (somapacitan-beco) which are approved for pediatric growth hormone deficiency and may be a good option for patients experienced with the daily GH products.

This calculator aims to simplify GH dosing by automatically filtering the calculated results based on disease indication and product. Per the Pediatric Endocrine Society's 2024 Statement Update, there is no preferred somatropin product. This calculator is not intended to provide patient-individualized product recommendations.
